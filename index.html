Notre Histoire

Rooted in Québec
since 1987

What began as a small casse-croûte tucked along the cobblestones of Rue Saint-Louis has grown into one of Vieux-Québec's most beloved traditions. Three generations of the Beaumont family have kept the same recipe: hand-cut fries, a brown gravy simmered for eight hours, and cheese curds sourced fresh every morning from a fromagerie in Warwick.

We believe poutine is not fast food — it is Québécois soul food. Each bowl is assembled à la minute, and we will never compromise on the squeak.
